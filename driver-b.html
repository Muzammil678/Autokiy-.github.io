Documents required include but are not limited to: Driving license, national ID card, vehicle registration documents, insurance, recent profile picture.

The auto inspection varies by market. We will either do the auto check ourselves (by the city teams) or use 3rd party services that do the auto check for us like Nacita in Egypt and CarSure in Pakistan.
